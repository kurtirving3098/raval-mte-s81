ABOUT US

The Internet is worth Remembering

We at the Museum of Internet History believe that the Internet is worth preserving. Servers shut down, files get corrupted, and websites are left in disrepair.

Our mission is to save as much of the internet's history as we can — to show future generations what the old internet looked like, how it felt, and why it mattered.

We are starting with Filipino internet culture: the Friendster profiles, the PinoyExchange threads, the Geocities-era homepages. Every link, every image, every post is a piece of our shared digital heritage.
